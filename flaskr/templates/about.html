About Us

We made this website to help the thousands of people who need blood and provide them with an easy and seemless experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to give our users the ability to search their specific blood type and try to find nearby donors. We also give the ability to contact the donors. Allowing you to easily find matches near you provides a seamless and intuitive process. We hope this will make finding donor matches easier and effective countrywide.

Designers

Michael Jones and Mikey Chowyen are both Computer Science majors at Southern Connecticut State University.
